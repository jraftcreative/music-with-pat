Meet Your Teacher

About Patricia — Piano Teacher in Tengah, Singapore

Patricia is a piano teacher based in Tengah, Singapore with over 10 years of experience teaching young children from age four and up. She believes that every child learns differently and grows at their own pace, and her teaching approach is gentle, patient, and encouraging.

With a Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education with Music Education, Patricia thoughtfully designs lessons that feel safe, enjoyable, and meaningful for young learners. Through fun and creative activities, children are guided to build confidence, develop musical understanding, and gradually cultivate their musical potential.

She offers ABRSM syllabus preparation where appropriate, and lessons take place in a calm, home-based studio in Tengah, Singapore where children feel comfortable, supported, and free to grow.

What Sets Patricia Apart

Child-Centred Approach

Every lesson is tailored to your child's learning style, pace, and personality. No cookie-cutter methods here.

Education Background

With a B.Ed in Early Childhood & Music Education, Patricia understands how young minds develop and learn best.

Nurturing Environment

Lessons take place in a warm, home-based studio in Tengah — convenient for families in Jurong West, Bukit Batok, Bukit Timah, and Choa Chu Kang.
