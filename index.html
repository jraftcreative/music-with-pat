About

Meet Patricia

Patricia is a piano teacher based in Tengah, Singapore with over 10 years of experience teaching young children from age four and up. Her teaching approach is gentle, patient, and encouraging.

With a Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education with Music Education, Patricia designs lessons that feel safe, enjoyable, and meaningful for young learners. Her home studio is conveniently located for families in Tengah, Jurong West, Bukit Batok, Bukit Timah, and Choa Chu Kang.
