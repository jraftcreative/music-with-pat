What We Offer

Piano Lessons for Children in Tengah, Singapore

Patricia's lessons are thoughtfully structured to nurture your child's musical growth — from first notes to ABRSM examinations and beyond. Conveniently located in Tengah, west Singapore.

Ages 4 and Up

Lessons are designed for young learners from age four, when musical foundations are most naturally absorbed. Complete beginners are always welcome.

45–60 Minute Sessions

Each lesson covers both theory and practical work. Session length is tailored to your child's age and attention span.

Home-Based Studio

Lessons take place in Patricia's calm, comfortable home studio in Tengah, Singapore. House visits are available for families in Jurong West, Bukit Batok, Bukit Timah, Choa Chu Kang, and surrounding areas.

ABRSM Preparation

Where appropriate, students are prepared for ABRSM graded examinations. Patricia guides each student through the syllabus at a comfortable pace.

Lesson Structure

What a Typical Lesson Looks Like

5–10 min

Warm-Up and Technique Exercises

Scales, finger exercises, and posture check to start focused.

20–25 min

Repertoire and Sight-Reading

Working through pieces, learning to read music fluently, and developing expression.

10–15 min

Theory and Aural Training

Understanding musical concepts, ear training, and rhythm exercises.

5 min

Review and Practice Goals

Setting clear, achievable goals for home practice.

Patricia adapts each lesson based on your child's energy and engagement level. Some days call for more creative exploration, while others are perfect for focused technique work.

Tailored Approach

Lessons by Age Group

Ages 4–6

Early Starters

Fun musical activities, rhythm games, basic notation, short attention-friendly segments, no exam pressure.

Tailored activities that feel like play while building real musical foundations.

Ages 7–9

Building Skills

ABRSM Grades 1–3 preparation, sight-reading development, theory fundamentals, building performance confidence.

A structured approach that balances challenge with encouragement.

Ages 10–12

Growing Musicians

ABRSM Grades 3–5+, expressive playing, complex repertoire, developing musicality and interpretation.

Students begin to develop their own musical identity.

Location

Conveniently Located in Tengah

Patricia's home studio is located in Tengah Garden Town, Singapore's newest car-free HDB town. The quiet, family-friendly environment is ideal for focused learning without distractions.

The studio is easily accessible from Jurong West, Bukit Batok, Bukit Timah, Choa Chu Kang, and surrounding neighbourhoods in west Singapore. The future Jurong Region Line MRT will improve access further.

House visits are also available for families who prefer their child to learn at home. Contact Patricia to discuss arrangements.
